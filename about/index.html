Hi. I'm Shiva. I come from a family of physicians where everyone was a doctor and everything was just too scientific around me. Amids this scientific environment, since childhood, i've been looking for a way to express my emotions. It is my desire to give life to emotions in the form of watercolor on paper. I illustrate through the lens of my emotions anything and everything i see around me; the sky and its clouds, the city and its people. Though I studied medicine at the university, for the past few years I have considered a different type of therapy, one that lies closer to my true passion, therapy through watercolor art. I particularly enjoy painting urban landscapes; the city skylines, the buildings, the streets. On a technical level, my style of watercolor is wet on wet which allows for fluid, fun and unpredictable effects on paper.
